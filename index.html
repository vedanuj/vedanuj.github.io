Vedanuj Goswami

I am a software engineer(machine learning) in the Facebook AI and Machine Perception research team under Fernando De La Torre, working in computer vision and deep learning. Here at Facebook I have worked on developing efficient computational models for image and video analysis for Mobile AR and VR like face tracking, recognition and semantic scene understanding.

I contribute to open source DL frameworks like Pytorch, Glow.

I did my MS in Machine Learning at Georgia Tech, where I was advised by Prof. Ashok Goel. Before my MS, I worked briefly at Samsung Research in Camera R&D. I did my bachelors in Computer Science from NITS, India where I was advised by Prof. Samir Borgohain.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, natural language processing and machine learning. Much of my work till now has been on deep learning, semantic scene understanding, natural language processing. I have also worked in artificial immune networks.

Only Time Can Tell: Discovering Temporal Data for Temporal Modeling
Laura Sevilla-Lara, Shengxin Zha, Zhicheng Yan, Vedanuj Goswami, Matt Feiszli, Lorenzo Torresani
arXiv preprint arXiv:1907.08340, 2019
arxiv / bibtex

A methodology to discover the relevance of temporal information in action classes and use it to identify categories that contain more temporal information (temporal classes). These set of classes are used to benchmark current video models, revealing a network's ability to model temporal information and also for training that boosts accuracy on the temporal categories.
Knowledge Extraction and Annotation for Cross-Domain Textual Case-Based Reasoning in Biologically Inspired Design

International Conference on Case Based Reasoning (ICCBR), 2016
pdf / blog / poster / bibtex

Automated NLP based technique for extracting partial SBF (Structure, Behavior, Function) models of biological systems from natural language documents for potential use in biologically inspired design.
